# Org Planning

Use this workflow for headcount planning, org design, sequencing hires, and evaluating team structure.

## Planning Dimensions

- headcount by role and timing
- reporting lines and boundaries
- sequencing of hires
- cost and budget tradeoffs

## Useful Benchmarks

Consider:

- span of control
- management layers
- IC-to-manager ratio
- team size

Use benchmarks as prompts, not hard rules.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the planning question.
2. Understand the current org and target outcomes.
3. Identify structural pain points or single points of failure.
4. Model hiring sequence and role priorities.
5. Provide a text-based org-plan recommendation with tradeoffs.

## Output

Produce:

- headcount plan
- role-priority list
- org-structure notes
- flagged risks such as management overhead or fragile dependencies

## Guardrails

- Avoid pretending org heuristics are universal.
- Explain the tradeoffs behind each recommendation.
- Separate structural issues from near-term staffing gaps.
