# Capacity Planning

Assess team capacity, utilization, bottlenecks, and scenarios for staffing or reprioritization.

## Inputs

Gather:

- team size and roles
- current workload
- upcoming work
- constraints such as budget, hiring lead time, or skill gaps

## Planning Dimensions

- people
- budget
- time

## Guidance

- target utilization below 100 percent
- account for meetings, support work, PTO, and interruptions
- distinguish project work from BAU and reactive load

## Output

Produce:

- current utilization table
- total capacity summary
- upcoming demand table
- bottlenecks
- recommendations
- scenario analysis

## Connected-Source Enhancements

- project trackers can provide workload and upcoming commitments
- calendars can reveal actual availability and recurring meeting load
