# Vendor Review

Evaluate a vendor using structured analysis across total cost, risk, performance, and strategic fit.

## Inputs

Gather:

- vendor name
- evaluation context
- contract, proposal, or pricing details
- any known performance history

## Review Framework

### Cost Analysis

Assess:

- licensing or subscription cost
- implementation and migration
- onboarding and training
- support and maintenance
- exit or switching costs

### Risk Assessment

Assess:

- financial stability
- security and compliance posture
- dependency or concentration risk
- lock-in and exit terms
- business continuity and recovery posture

### Performance And Fit

Review:

- SLAs
- support responsiveness
- reliability
- feature delivery cadence
- customer satisfaction or internal stakeholder feedback

## Output

Produce:

- summary recommendation
- cost analysis table
- risk matrix
- strengths
- concerns
- recommendation
- negotiation points

## Connected-Source Enhancements

- use a knowledge base for prior evaluations, policies, and contracts
- use procurement or contract systems when available for spend and renewal context
