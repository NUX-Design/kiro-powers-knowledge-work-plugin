# Status Reporting

Generate concise leadership or stakeholder reports with metrics, accomplishments, risks, and decisions needed.

## Output Structure

Produce:

- executive summary
- overall status indicator
- KPI table
- accomplishments
- in-progress work
- risks and issues
- decisions needed
- next-period priorities

## Reporting Principles

- lead with the headline
- highlight what is at risk, not just what is complete
- make decision asks explicit and actionable
- provide context and recommendations, not just raw updates

## Connected-Source Enhancements

- project trackers provide milestones, overdue items, and progress
- chat systems surface blockers and decisions
- calendars help anchor the reporting period with key meetings and milestones
