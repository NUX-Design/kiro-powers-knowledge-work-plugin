# Risk Assessment

Identify, prioritize, and mitigate operational risks using a structured register.

## Risk Matrix

Classify risk levels using likelihood and impact:

- Low
- Medium
- High
- Critical

## Common Categories

- operational
- financial
- compliance
- strategic
- reputational
- security

## Risk Register Fields

For each risk capture:

- description
- likelihood
- impact
- risk level
- mitigation
- owner
- status

## Output

Produce a prioritized risk register focused on material and controllable risks, with actionable mitigations and clear ownership.
