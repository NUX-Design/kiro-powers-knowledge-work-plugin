# Audit Support

Support SOX 404 and audit preparation with control testing methodology, sampling approaches, documentation standards, and deficiency evaluation.

This workflow assists with audit support but does not provide legal or audit opinions.

## SOX 404 Methodology

1. Scope significant accounts and assertions.
2. Assess risk of material misstatement.
3. Identify controls addressing each risk.
4. Test design and operating effectiveness.
5. Evaluate deficiencies and compensating controls.
6. Document management assessment and conclusions.

## Significant Account Factors

Consider:

- quantitative materiality
- transaction volume
- complexity
- fraud susceptibility
- management judgment
- prior misstatements

## Testing Concepts

### Design Effectiveness

Determine whether the control is placed correctly and addresses the intended risk.

### Operating Effectiveness

Determine whether the control actually operated as designed throughout the reliance period.

## Sampling Approaches

- random selection
- targeted or judgmental selection
- haphazard selection when random is impractical
- systematic selection across a population

Increase sample size when risk is higher, deficiencies existed previously, or external auditors rely on the work.

## Workpaper Requirements

Every workpaper should document:

- control ID and description
- control type and frequency
- risk and assertion
- test objective and procedures
- expected evidence
- population and sample method
- per-sample results
- exception details
- conclusion and sign-off

## Evidence Standards

Prefer dated, attributable, corroborated evidence such as audit logs, approvals, screenshots, and reperformed calculations. Avoid unsupported verbal statements as sole evidence.

## Deficiency Classification

- Deficiency
- Significant Deficiency
- Material Weakness

Evaluate likelihood, magnitude, control importance, and compensating controls, including aggregation of multiple smaller issues.

## Output

Produce:

- scoping notes
- control testing methodology
- sample strategy
- workpaper structure
- deficiency evaluation guidance
