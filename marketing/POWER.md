# Marketing

Guided marketing power for campaign execution, content production, brand consistency, performance reporting, SEO work, and lifecycle messaging. It works standalone when you provide drafts, URLs, campaign context, or metrics directly, and it becomes more powerful when connected to design, analytics, SEO, and marketing platform MCP tools.

## Steering Index

- `steering/content-drafting.md`: Draft blogs, social posts, newsletters, landing pages, press releases, and case studies.
- `steering/content-frameworks.md`: Reference content templates, writing best practices, SEO basics, and CTA patterns.
- `steering/campaign-planning.md`: Build full campaign briefs with messaging, channel strategy, calendar, and KPIs.
- `steering/brand-review.md`: Review copy against brand voice, terminology, style rules, and risk flags.
- `steering/competitive-analysis.md`: Compare competitors across positioning, messaging, content, and market signals.
- `steering/performance-reporting.md`: Turn channel or campaign metrics into executive summaries and prioritized actions.
- `steering/seo-audits.md`: Audit SEO health, keyword opportunities, technical issues, and competitor gaps.
- `steering/email-sequences.md`: Design lifecycle email flows with full copy, timing, branching, and benchmarks.

## When To Use Which Workflow

- Use `content-drafting.md` when you need new marketing copy for a specific channel or format.
- Use `content-frameworks.md` when you want reusable structures, headline formulas, or SEO and CTA guidance.
- Use `campaign-planning.md` when translating a goal into an executable campaign brief and calendar.
- Use `brand-review.md` before publishing to catch voice drift, terminology issues, weak claims, or compliance risks.
- Use `competitive-analysis.md` when reassessing positioning, building battlecards, or reacting to competitor moves.
- Use `performance-reporting.md` when summarizing campaign or channel results for stakeholders.
- Use `seo-audits.md` when analyzing rankings, content gaps, on-page issues, or technical SEO priorities.
- Use `email-sequences.md` when creating onboarding, nurture, launch, win-back, or re-engagement flows.

## Standalone Versus Supercharged

This power works without MCP:

- Paste drafts, URLs, messaging notes, campaign goals, or performance metrics directly.
- Ask for reports, outlines, briefs, or ready-to-use copy in plain language.
- Provide screenshots or summarized dashboard numbers when direct integrations are unavailable.

With MCP connected, it can work with richer context:

- Design tools: access brand assets and design files.
- Marketing automation and email tools: align campaign and lifecycle workflows to platform behavior.
- Analytics tools: pull marketing and product performance data for reporting.
- SEO and traffic tools: enrich keyword research and competitive analysis.
- Knowledge tools: access briefs, style guides, and campaign documents.

See `mcp.json` for optional integrations. Every included server is disabled by default so you can opt in explicitly.

## Best Practices

- Provide the audience, objective, tone, and distribution channel up front so outputs stay aligned.
- Treat generated copy as a draft and apply brand, legal, and factual review before publishing.
- Use benchmarks and historical data when setting targets or judging performance.
- Keep competitive and SEO analyses current; these degrade quickly if source data is stale.
- Tie recommendations back to the actual goal, not just channel best practices.
