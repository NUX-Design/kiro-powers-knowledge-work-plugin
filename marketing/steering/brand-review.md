# Brand Review

Review content against brand voice, style rules, messaging pillars, and compliance-sensitive phrasing.

## Inputs

- content to review
- brand guidelines source, if available

If no brand guide is available, fall back to a general review for clarity, consistency, and professionalism.

## Review Dimensions

### Voice And Tone

- consistency with defined voice attributes
- fit for audience and channel
- abrupt tonal shifts

### Terminology And Language

- preferred versus avoided terms
- correct product naming
- jargon level
- capitalization and formatting conventions

### Messaging Alignment

- support for core value propositions
- consistency with positioning
- contradictions or dilution

### Style Guide Compliance

- grammar and punctuation rules
- formatting conventions
- heading and list patterns
- number and date formatting

### Legal And Compliance Flags

Always check for:

- unsubstantiated claims
- missing disclaimers
- risky competitor comparisons
- regulated-industry language
- testimonial issues
- likely copyright concerns

## Output

```markdown
### Summary
- overall assessment
- biggest strengths
- most important improvements

### Detailed Findings
| Issue | Location | Severity | Suggestion |
|-------|----------|----------|------------|
```

Severity:

- High
- Medium
- Low
